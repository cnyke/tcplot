Twin Cities Data Visualization Community

What we do

tcplot() brings together data practitioners, designers, artists, and the data-curious in Minneapolis–St. Paul. We learn from each other through hands-on events about how to see, shape, and tell stories with data.

Talks

Short presentations from local practitioners on tools, techniques, and data stories from the Twin Cities and beyond.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions where you build something real — from your first chart to advanced interactive visualizations.

Build-a-thons

Collaborative making sessions around a shared dataset or theme. Come with a laptop, leave with a viz.
